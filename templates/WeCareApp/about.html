Welcome to WeCare

WeCare provides free and confidential emotional support to people in suicidal crisis or emotional distress 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, across India. The Lifeline is comprised of a national network of over 150 local crisis centers, combining custom local care and resources with national standards and best practices.
